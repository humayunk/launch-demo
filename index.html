The Critical Insight

Decouple design
from implementation.

The skill doesn't try to reskin the app in the same session.

It produces DESIGN.md — a complete spec capturing every token, component, and decision.

A separate Claude Code agent runs the reskin in parallel while I keep iterating on brand and marketing.

One conversation per concern. Specs are the handoff.
