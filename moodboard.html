Brand Exploration

Cortex

Exploring the visual identity for Cortex — a knowledge library for curious people.

A visual knowledge library with AI-generated illustrated concept cards and spaced repetition learning.

Brand Personality

Five words that define the brand

Curious

Not passive consumption. Active exploration. The product invites you to go deeper, to ask "what else?"

Visual

Knowledge made tangible through illustration. Every concept gets a face. Memory anchored in imagery.

Warm

Not clinical, not cold. Learning should feel like a good bookshop, not a lecture hall.

Considered

Every detail is intentional. The restraint of a well-edited magazine. Quiet confidence in craft.

Confident

It knows what it is. No trend-chasing. The design speaks with authority without raising its voice.

Target Personas

The Lifelong Learner

Takes online courses, reads nonfiction, listens to podcasts. Has a notes app full of highlights they never revisit. Wants to actually retain what they learn, not just consume it.

The Visual Thinker

Thinks in pictures, diagrams, spatial relationships. Text-heavy flashcard apps feel dead to them. Needs concepts anchored in imagery to make knowledge stick.

The Taste-Driven Autodidact

Cares about tools as much as outcomes. Uses Bear, Notion, or Obsidian. Wants their knowledge tool to feel as considered as the rest of their stack.
